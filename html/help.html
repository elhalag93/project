Placing an Order on Eshtry.com

1. Log in to your Eshtry.com account using your registered email address and password. If you do not have an account yet, click here on how to register on Eshtry.com.
2. Search for the product you want to purchase.
3. Check the product details, images, specifications, videos, and customer reviews. Choose the item and click on Add to Cart.
4. Review the product details and click on Proceed to Checkout.
5. Fill in your shipping address.
6. Review the address, shipping services selected and delivery period.
7. Select the payment method and complete your purchase by clicking on Place Order.
8. You will be provided with an order number along with an order tracking link for you to track your orde

Items You Ordered

To return a product you have purchased: 1. Login to your Souq.com account and click on your name displayed on the top right-hand corner of the page.
2. Click on My Orders from the drop-down menu.
3. Locate the order you want to return and click on Return Item.
Return an item 4. Select the checkbox against the product you wish to return, choose the reason for the return from drop-down menu, and add notes in the box stating the reason for the return and click on Complete Return.
Note: Provide as much information as possible before completing your return as the product you return will be assessed and validated, before it is accepted for a refund. It takes 3-5 working days after the return request for the courier to pick up the item and up to 5 business days for us or the seller to receive and process your return.
Note: Timings given exclude Fridays, Saturdays and Public holidays / Fridays and Public holidays for shipping company. If you want to reschedule the pick up or change the pickup address or phone number, please contact us. If the courier calls you on your registered phone number asking to pick up the item and you wish to reschedule, you can inform the courier. Before returning any electronic devices that have your personal data stored, remove any passwords or security features, accessories (Memory card, cover, etc.) from the device.
